Who We Are

A Brand Born from Ancient Roots

OpenTree is a spiritual wellness brand inspired by the timeless wisdom of Eastern philosophy. We believe that the ancient practices of our ancestors — their relationship with nature, energy, and stillness — hold the keys to modern wellbeing.

Our products are carefully crafted to bring these ancient rituals into your everyday life. From the first sip of our Sacred Tea at dawn, to the quiet glow of a Zen Candle at dusk, every OpenTree product is designed to guide you back to yourself.

We source our ingredients ethically, work with artisan craftspeople, and create each product with intention. OpenTree is more than a brand — it is a practice, a community, and a path toward inner awakening.
