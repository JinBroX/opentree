Who We Are

A Brand Born from Ancient Roots

OpenTree is a spiritual wellness brand inspired by the timeless wisdom of Eastern philosophy. We believe that the ancient practices of our ancestors — their relationship with nature, energy, and stillness — hold the keys to modern wellbeing.

Our products are carefully crafted to bring these ancient rituals into your everyday life. From the first sip of our Sacred Agarwood Tea at dawn, to the quiet energy of a Bian stone bracelet worn close to the body.

We source our ingredients ethically, work with artisan craftspeople across East Asia, and create each product with intention and reverence.
